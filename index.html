Getting to SLC 2015

The easiest way to travel to Berkeley is to fly into either San Francisco (SFO) or Oakland (OAK) airport. Berkeley is a short taxi or BART ride away.

In Berkeley, stay at Bancroft Hotel or Hotel Shattuck Plaza. These hotels are walking distance from the conference. Mention the "university discount" and that you are part of the SLC 2015 conference for a rate discount.

IEEE-HKN can reimburse up to US$250 per Chapter to help offset travel expenses. Have questions about funding your trip? Send an e-mail to info@hkn.org to get in touch.

Stay tuned for more updates on this page, and the IEEE-HKN Facebook page.
